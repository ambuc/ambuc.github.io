> Let $A = {1,2,3}$. What are all the subsets of $A$? Hint: there are 8.  

Any element $a$ either _is_ or _is not_ included in a subset $A' \in A$. With
two choices per element, there are $2^\abs{A} = 2^3 = 8$ possible subsets.
