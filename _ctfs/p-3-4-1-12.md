> Let $G = (V, A, \text{src}, \text{tgt})$ be the graph below.
>
> $$
> \require{AMScd}
> \begin{CD}
>   @.   b @<<<    c                   \\
> @.     @|        @|                  \\
> a @>>> b @>>> c  @>>>            d   \\
> @.     @.        @. \diaguparrow{}{} \\
> e @<<< f @>>>    g
> \end{CD}
> $$
>
> In the corresponding pre-preorder which of the following are true:
>
> _a)_ $a \leqslant b$?
> _b)_ $a \leqslant c$?
> _c)_ $c \leqslant b$?
> _d)_ $b = c$?
> _e)_ $e \leqslant f$?
> _f)_ $f \leqslant d$?
