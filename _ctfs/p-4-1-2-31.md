>
> $$ v_0 \stackrel{e}\to v_1 $$
>

> Example 4.1.2.30). We call $[1]$ the _free arrow category_.
>
> _a)_ What are its objects?
>
> _b)_ For every pair of objects in $[1]$, write down the hom-set.
