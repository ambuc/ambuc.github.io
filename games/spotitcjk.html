SpotItCJK
Instructions

This game is a lot like Dobble, except with subcomponents of CJK characters. There will always be exactly one pair of symbols (one symbol in blue from the left-hand card, one symbol in yellow from the right-hand card) such that the two symbols share a subcomponent.

Example: 薄 and 淩 share the subcomponent 氵.
